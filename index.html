Our Story

Cooked with Passion, Served with Pride

Salt'n Pepper started as a small dream right here in Bahawalpur — to give this city a place where families could sit together and enjoy real, honest food without any fuss. We believe in using fresh ingredients, traditional spice blends, and recipes passed down through years of cooking experience.

Located at Haqi Centre, DC Chowk, we have been serving Bahawalpur since day one. Whether you come in for a quick lunch or a long dinner with your family, we always make sure you leave happy and full.
